Our project

Our project is to research methods of power generation involving generators, and to build a small hand-powered generator. Power generation is an important part of modern civilization: massive amounts of electricity fuel innovation and make tasks once thought to be incredibly difficult or impossible trivial.

We wish to use this project to explore some methods of power generation, especially those involving generators, and show how varied these methods can be, with everything from turning a hand crank to operating a massive nuclear power plant.
